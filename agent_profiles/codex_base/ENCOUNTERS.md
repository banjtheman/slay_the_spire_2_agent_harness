# Encounter Notes

These notes are optional harness knowledge. They should be short, tactical, and
biased toward the next action. Match sections by enemy name, ids, powers, status
cards, or mechanic names.

## General Boss Rules

Triggers: boss

- Boss fights punish slow generic HP preservation. Identify the boss-specific
  clock, scaling, summons, or invulnerability rule first.
- If the boss has an alternate loss condition or hard timer, damage and card
  velocity usually beat incremental blocking.
- Use draw and generation potions early in boss fights while energy remains.
  Do not wait until after energy is spent to use a draw potion.
- If a defensive line does not prevent death, prefer the line that creates the
  most chance to win immediately or next turn.

## The Insatiable

Aliases: THE_INSATIABLE, THE_INSATIABLE_BOSS
Triggers: Sandpit, SANDPIT_POWER, Frantic Escape, FRANTIC_ESCAPE

- This fight is a damage race. Sandpit is a hard instant-death timer, not a
  normal incoming-damage problem.
- The boss adds Frantic Escape cards. Playing one increases Sandpit by 1, but
  it costs energy and its cost increases each time it is played. Escapes buy
  time but also slow the kill.
- Do not play Frantic Escape just because it is playable. If Sandpit is 3 or
  higher, spend energy on strong attacks, Vulnerable, Strength, or draw unless
  there is no meaningful offensive play.
- Prefer playing Frantic Escape when Sandpit is 1-2, when the next boss turn
  would eat you, or after the main damage line if enough energy remains.
- Avoid playing multiple Frantic Escapes in the same turn unless Sandpit is
  critical; repeated cost increases can make later escapes unusable.
- The opening non-attack/status turns are setup and burst windows. Play scaling
  and high damage early instead of blocking.
- Treat Frantic Escape as lower priority than a strong lethal-race line such as
  One-Two Punch into a large attack, Bludgeon, Fiend Fire, or Vulnerable plus
  multiple attacks.
- If HP is low but the boss still has high HP, blocking can be necessary, but
  do not spend energy on Frantic Escape while Sandpit is already safe and the
  hand contains meaningful damage.
